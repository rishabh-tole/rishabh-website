MapPin'

Accessibility Audio Integration Haptic Feedback Mobile Dev

A visual, tactile, and auditory map to aid people who struggle with dyslexia and visual impairments.

Overview

MapPin' is designed to make navigation accessible for everyone. It integrates multi-sensory feedback loops to aid users who may find traditional visual maps difficult to interpret.

How it Works

The application uses a combination of high-contrast visuals, auditory cues (voice guidance and distinct sound alerts), and haptic feedback (vibrations) to guide users. This multimodal approach ensures that information is conveyed through multiple channels, reducing cognitive load and increasing accessibility.

Impact

By providing alternative ways to perceive spatial information, MapPin' empowers users with dyslexia and visual impairments to navigate their environments with greater independence and confidence.
