Pingin'

Next.js PostgreSQL AWS Agentic AI

Agentic AI in college admission consulting; received 50k first round funding and working on scaling.

The Problem

College consulting is becoming incredibly expensive, creating a significant barrier for students from lower economic backgrounds. Access to high-quality advice and mentorship should not be a luxury. I wanted to democratize this process and provide high-quality, personalized guidance to everyone, regardless of their financial situation.

The Solution

Pingin' is a comprehensive platform where students can submit essays to real mentors and receive quick, actionable feedback through our "ping a mentor" system. We utilize proprietary AI, trained on hundreds of successful essays, to provide instant analysis and suggestions.

Additionally, we leverage Agentic AI to automate parts of the admissions process, such as finding relevant professors for research opportunities and drafting personalized outreach emails to help students build their profiles.

Technical Highlights

  • Next.js & React: Built a responsive and dynamic frontend for a seamless user experience.
  • PostgreSQL & Alembic: Robust relational database management with automated schema migrations.
  • AWS Infrastructure: Scalable cloud hosting and storage solutions.
