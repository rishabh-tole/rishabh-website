SedLev

IoT Data Analysis Environmental Tech Machine Learning

Sediment Level Detection system to prevent water pollution and street flooding by monitoring storm drain basins.

The Challenge

Storm drains are essential for managing urban water runoff, but they often get clogged with sediment and debris. Manual inspection is costly, labor-intensive, and often reactive rather than proactive. Delayed maintenance can lead to street flooding during heavy rains and environmental pollution if contaminants are not removed.

The Solution

SedLev is an IoT-based system designed to automatically monitor sediment levels within catch basins. It utilizes sensors to measure the accumulation of solids and transmits this data to a central dashboard.

Impact

By automating the monitoring process, municipalities can optimize their cleaning schedules, dispatching crews only when necessary. This leads to significant cost savings, reduced carbon footprint from maintenance vehicles, and better prevention of environmental mishaps.
